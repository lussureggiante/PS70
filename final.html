The condition, also known as congenital amusia, leads to difficulty perceiving pitch differences and generating hedonic responses, ultimately affecting their interconnection with the social and cultural significance of music. ENSEMBLE is a wearable device that employs sensory substitution technology. By translating sound and music into visual and haptic feedback, it creates a new language of perception that opens the door to new forms of communication and expression. ENSEMBLE allows the users to interact with the surrounding soundscape, amplifying and manipulating their awareness of their immediate environment. It is designed to enable new sensory explorations and foster greater understanding and empathy toward those who experience the world differently. ENSEMBLE is also a powerful tool for interrogation and introspection. By enveloping the users in a symphony of sensations, it challenges the traditional hierarchy of senses.
